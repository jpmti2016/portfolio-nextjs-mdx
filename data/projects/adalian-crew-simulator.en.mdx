Influence crew builder help optimize the crew composition to get the best results in your activities while playing the [Influence](https://www.influenceth.io/) blockchain game.

Built with `NextJS`, `Tailwind UI` and the `influenceth/sdk`.

Focus in using `Tailwind UI` components, form, tables, ReactJS state and api calls.
